Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

The short version

MoodSketch does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Everything you draw stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. MoodSketch stores your sketches and mood tags locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any server. No account is required. No analytics are collected.

Local storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save:

  • Your daily sketches (stored as image data)
  • Mood tags you've added to sketches
  • Your streak count and last draw date
  • Your onboarding status and reminder time preference

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser data will delete it permanently.

Third-party services

MoodSketch does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

Children's privacy

MoodSketch does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect data, we don't anticipate meaningful changes.
